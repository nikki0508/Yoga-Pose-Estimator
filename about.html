Yoga Pose Estimator

Hey there! Welcome to Yoga Pose Estimator, developed in 2021 by Nikita Uikey Student Of National Institute Of Technology, Delhi.

I created Yoga Pose Estimator inspired by various Machine Learning models for visual recognition. I have loved (almost) every second of this project and I have learnt more than I could have imagined.

Yoga Pose Estimator uses Pose.Net , developed by Google’s TensorFlow, and ml5.js , a JavaScript library for Machine Learning on the browser. Most of my learning was following The Coding Train’s YouTube playlist on Pose Estimation.
